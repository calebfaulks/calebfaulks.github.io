I'm Doing MTS!

MTS is a 2-year full-time ministry apprenticeship program which is all about growing in character, conviction and competency through constantly reading God's word, being thrown into loads of ministry roles and being mentored by a pastor, to ultimately become better equipped in every way for a life of serving Jesus.

Here's a short explanation by Scott Curtis on MTS and what it will look like for me.

Give Pray

Why MTS?

There are 3 main reasons I chose to do MTS:

I live for Jesus' glory. Over the past few years I have become more and more convicted of this truth. I exist to serve and glorify Jesus, and MTS is a way of doing that with all my time.

I want to be trained to serve Jesus well. Serving Jesus is hard. Not just practically, but mentally, emotionally and spiritually. I want to grow in my practical skills, relational skills and dependence on God.

I trust the MTS program. For over a decade, Hunter Bible Church has excelled at training apprentices and sending them out into full-time ministry. I have seen the results for myself and have already been experiencing this growth in my own walk with God during my apprenticeship.
